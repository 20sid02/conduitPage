Conduit

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Conduit is an offline-first iOS application developed by ArkSoft. The free version stores your information locally on your device and does not collect personal data.

Data Collection

Conduit does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or remotely process personal data.

The free version does not require an account and does not use analytics, advertising services, tracking technologies, cloud storage, or an external backend.

Information Stored on Your Device

Information entered into Conduit is stored locally on your device using Apple-provided storage technologies.

This information may include:

  • Client and project names
  • Deployment details
  • Service names and ports
  • Deployment URLs
  • Database configuration details
  • Usernames and administrative information
  • Custom notes and fields

ArkSoft cannot access, retrieve, or view the information you store inside Conduit.

Credentials and Sensitive Information

Sensitive values such as passwords, tokens, hosts, and credentials are stored separately using the iOS Keychain.

These credentials remain on your device and are not sent to ArkSoft or any external server.

Biometric Authentication

Conduit may use Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode to protect access to sensitive credentials.

Biometric authentication is handled entirely by iOS. Conduit does not receive, store, inspect, or transmit your biometric information.

Internet Access

Conduit does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

The app may open a deployment URL or another external link that you have manually saved. Any external website you open may have its own privacy policy and data practices.

Data Deletion

You can delete clients, deployments, custom fields, and related credentials from within Conduit.

Deleting the app removes its local application data. Keychain items are managed separately by iOS and may behave differently from ordinary application data. Conduit removes related Keychain credentials when supported deletion actions are performed inside the app.

Data Backup

Conduit does not operate its own backup or synchronization service.

Device-level backups may be handled by Apple according to your personal iCloud, device backup, and operating-system settings. ArkSoft does not control or access those backups.

Children’s Privacy

Conduit is not specifically directed toward children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Future Features

If a future version of Conduit introduces analytics, accounts, cloud synchronization, remote monitoring, or another feature that changes its data practices, this privacy policy will be updated before that feature is released.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated when Conduit’s features, legal requirements, or data practices change.

The effective date displayed at the top of this page will be updated whenever material changes are made.
